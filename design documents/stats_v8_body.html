Each unit lists its build cost (Weapon, Population) and battlefield statistics. For how training and combat work, see Rulebook § 5. Militia is an optional cheap defender — free upkeep and no movement, ideal for sitting on a city.

Edition VIII Balance Changes

Bomber nerfed. In playtest, Bombers as area weapons were too strong. The Bomber's stats are now:
  • Attack 1 (was 2), Range 1 (was 2). A Bomber now strikes a single ground unit at close range for modest damage.
  • Other stats unchanged: 5 weapons / 1 population / 3 HP / 2 speed / 5 fee.
  • Bombers no longer hit "all units in Range" — they target one unit at a time, like Planes.
Inherited from Edition VII:
  • Anti-Air Artillery range 4 (longest in the game).
  • Plane vs. ground damage –1.
  • Mechanized Infantry 3 weapons (up from 2).
  • Tanks and Boats speed 3.
  • Carriers: HP 5, speed 3.
  • Militia: 0 weapons, 2 population, 2 HP, free upkeep, cannot move.

Leaders & Unique Unit Traits

Pick one leader per nation at game start. Stuck with them for the whole game.
How to read these cards. Each nation lists 1–3 named leaders, tagged PASSIVE (always on), ACTIVE (declare on a turn, costs and cooldown), or HYBRID. Plus a Unique Unit Trait that modifies the nation's base units. Rules in Rulebook § 6.

Every 4 Factories you control produce +1 extra weapon per turn.

Passive: All German Tanks gain +1 Attack and +1 HP but cost +1 Weapon. Active (CD 6): Pay 2 gold per chosen Tank. Those Tanks gain +2 Speed for 2 turns.

Agendas, Policies & Plans

An optional draft deck of strategic doctrines. Still in design.

Each nation will hold three Agenda cards. The deck is optional and still in design. Each card lists a cost, an effect, a cooldown, and any precondition.

Worked Example · Germany's Blitzkrieg

⚡
Blitzkrieg — Germany
Cost: 1 gold per German Tank in play.
Effect: All German Tanks gain +2 Speed for one turn.
Cooldown: 6 turns.
Card Library — In Development. 54 cards total (3 × 18 nations). Provisional examples:
  • Soviet · Scorched Earth. Cost: 5 gold. Destroy improvements in one of your territories to deny them to an invader. Cooldown: 8 turns.
  • USA · Lend-Lease. Cost: 15 gold. Gift 10 weapons or 10 treasury to an ally. Cooldown: 4 turns.
  • Japan · Bushidō Charge. Cost: free. All Infantry +2 Attack for one turn; –1 HP next turn. Cooldown: 6 turns.
  • UK · Convoy Defense. Cost: 5 gold. Reveal one enemy naval region per turn for 3 turns. Cooldown: 5 turns.
  • China · Long March. Cost: free. Move any number of friendly units 2 tiles toward the interior without combat penalty. Cooldown: 10 turns.
